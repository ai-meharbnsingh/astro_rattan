## 1. Executive Summary: "The Facade of Precision"
The "Project 28 Astro App" presents a sophisticated UI but fails multiple "brutal" tests for astronomical and astrological accuracy. While some modules (Shadbala, Ashtakvarga) are implemented with high fidelity, the user-facing predictions are often driven by randomized templates, hardcoded data, or fundamentally flawed tables.

## 2. Vedic Calculation Audit

### A. Fundamental Errors in Avakhada Chakra (`app/avakhada_engine.py`)
*   **Gana Misassignments (CRITICAL):** The engine contains multiple errors in Gana (nature) assignments for Nakshatras.
    *   **Bharani (1):** Wrongly assigned to *Deva* (should be *Manushya*).
    *   **Anuradha (16):** Wrongly assigned to *Manushya* (should be *Deva*).
    *   **Jyeshtha (17):** Wrongly assigned to *Manushya* (should be *Rakshasa*).
    *   **Dhanishta (22):** Wrongly assigned to *Manushya* (should be *Rakshasa*).
    *   **Uttara Ashadha (20), Purva/Uttara Bhadrapada (24/25):** Wrongly assigned to *Rakshasa* (should be *Manushya*).
*   **Impact:** This invalidates the "Kundli Milan" (matching) scores, as Gana Koot is a major component (6 points).

### B. Rulership Contradictions (`app/horoscope_generator.py`)
*   **Western vs. Vedic Rulership:** The horoscope generator uses Pluto for Scorpio, Uranus for Aquarius, and Neptune for Pisces. This is a violation of Vedic (Jyotish) principles where Mars, Saturn, and Jupiter are the respective lords.
*   **Module Inconsistency:** This module contradicts the core `avakhada_engine.py` which correctly uses traditional Vedic lords.

### C. Horoscope Fraud
*   **Randomized Templates:** Daily/Weekly horoscopes are generated by randomly picking strings from `_CAREER`, `_LOVE`, and `_HEALTH` pools. There is **zero calculation** linking the current transits to these strings. It is purely aesthetic filler.

## 3. Lal Kitab Calculation Audit

### A. Advanced Engine Bypass
*   **Unused Depth:** `app/lalkitab_advanced.py` implements complex "Masnui Grah" and "Karmic Debts," but these are sequestered in an "Advanced" route. The primary user dashboards use a separate, much softer logic.
*   **Sleeping Planets Logic:** While implemented, it is not factored into the "Confidence Scores" or "Career Predictions" returned by the main routes.

### B. Simplified Mappings
*   **Career/Health/Wealth Routes:** Driven by static dictionaries (`_CAREER_MAP`, `_HEALTH_MAP`) that map a single planet in a house to a static prediction. This ignores:
    *   Planetary strength (Shadbala).
    *   Aspects (Lal Kitab Drishti).
    *   Conjunctions (Masnui planets).
